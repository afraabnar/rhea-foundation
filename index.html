Our Mission

Standing Together, Rising Together

Rhea Mothers Alliance Foundation is a grassroots organization dedicated to uplifting mothers across underserved communities. We believe that when a mother is empowered — with knowledge, support, and community — entire families are transformed.

Through targeted programs, advocacy efforts, and community-building initiatives, we address the real, everyday challenges that mothers face and provide pathways toward greater stability and well-being.

Compassion-firstEvery mother's story is unique. We lead with empathy and listen before we act.

Community-rootedOur projects are shaped by the mothers we serve, not imposed from the outside.

SisterhoodNo mother walks alone in our network. We build bonds that last beyond our programs.

"Named after Rhea — the ancient mother of the gods — our foundation carries forward the power of maternal strength across generations."

Our Work

Projects Under Our Umbrella

Each project targets a specific need — together they form a safety net for mothers at every stage.

Health & Wellness

MamaWell

A maternal health initiative providing free prenatal and postnatal support, mental health resources, and wellness workshops to mothers in underserved communities. MamaWell ensures no mother faces pregnancy or early motherhood without guidance and care.

280+ Mothers enrolled
8 Partner clinics
2021 Founded
Education & Skills

Rise & Lead

An economic empowerment program offering skills training, micro-business mentorship, and financial literacy workshops tailored for mothers re-entering the workforce or building their own path toward financial independence.

220+ Graduates
15 Mentors
2023 Founded
